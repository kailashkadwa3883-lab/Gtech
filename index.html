Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions from our customers — answered clearly.

Q
Can I watch my CCTV cameras on my mobile phone?
Yes, all our modern IP and HD camera systems support remote live viewing on Android and iOS devices via a secure app.
Q
Do you provide a warranty on installation and products?
Yes, we provide standard manufacturer warranties on all products (cameras, DVRs, alarms) along with post-installation support.
Q
What is an AMC, and do I need it?
AMC stands for Annual Maintenance Contract. It includes regular health checks and servicing of your security system to prevent sudden failures.
Q
How long does installation take?
A standard 4-camera home setup takes 3–4 hours. Larger commercial installations may take a full day. We schedule at your convenience.
Q
Do you offer free site surveys?
Yes! Our expert technicians visit your site, assess the layout, and recommend the best camera placement — completely free of charge.
Q
Which brands do you deal in?
We are authorised dealers of Hikvision, Dahua, CP Plus, Samsung, and Honeywell — all 100% original products with valid Indian warranty.
